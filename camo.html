CamoQuest

Our second IP furthest in development currently is CamoQuest.

Inspired by GameBoy games of old, view the world like never before as you play as a young, wide-eyed girl.
During the day, her life stays in a dark, dreary GameBoy green palette.



But at night, in her dreams, her imagination LIGHTS UP not just her mind, but the colors as well!
Explore a fantasy world like no other when CamoQuest releases soon.

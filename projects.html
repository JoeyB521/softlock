Project ISO

Project ISO is an upcoming 3D Platformer game starring an unlikely hero in space.
Explore a desolate solar system, where you are the only inhabitant. You must search not only for your people,
but a way to reach them too. Stay tuned for more information regarding this title (and its release), in the future.

Ataraxia

Ataraxia is a first-person survival horror game set in a city at the bottom of the ocean, surrounded by a giant glass dome.
You are surrounded by your fellow cityfolk; however, there's also a body-snatching cryptid on the loose.
YOU CANT TRUST ANYONE.
You must make your way through the city as an electrical engineer, solving the city's power problem (and supernatural infestation).
Stay tuned for more information regarding this title (and its release), in the future.
